Getting Started

Does my child need any drama experience?
Absolutely not. Every class at Drama Geeks welcomes complete beginners. We have students who've never performed in front of anyone, and within a term they're on a stage. Experience helps — but confidence, we find, comes first.
How do I book a free trial?
Simply fill in our booking form or email us directly. We'll confirm a date for your child to come along and try a class with no obligation.
What age can my child join?
We welcome children from age 4 (Mini Geeks) up to age 18 (Elite Geeks). If your child is 19 or older and interested in adult drama, get in touch — we may be able to help with recommendations.
My child is very shy. Is Drama Geeks right for them?
Drama Geeks is especially right for shy children. The performing arts environment — structured, supportive, non-judgmental — is one of the best places a shy child can be. Many of our most confident alumni started as children who could barely look up when they walked in.

Classes

How are the classes structured?
Classes run every Saturday during term time. Each session follows a structured format: warm-up, skill-building, scene or production work, and a cool-down. The balance between these shifts depending on the group's age and what's happening that term.
What happens during a production term?
When a show is in production, the majority of class time is devoted to rehearsal. Parents are invited to the final performances — held at proper venues, with costumes, lighting and sound.
Can my child join mid-term?
We try to accommodate mid-term starts where possible. Get in touch and we'll advise based on where the group currently is in their term plan.
What if my child wants to move up a group?
Age transitions happen naturally. If a child is ready to move up early (or needs a little more time in their current group), we'll discuss it with you first. We always put the child's development first.

Payments & Admin

How and when do I pay?
Fees are payable termly in advance. We accept bank transfer, card payment and childcare vouchers. Full details are on our Fees page.
Is there a sibling discount?
Yes — we offer a discount for families with two or more children enrolled. See our Fees page for details.

Safety & Welfare

Are all the tutors DBS checked?
Yes — every Drama Geeks tutor holds a current Enhanced DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) certificate. These are renewed regularly. Full details are on our Safeguarding page.
What is your safeguarding policy?
We take safeguarding extremely seriously. We have a designated safeguarding lead, a full written policy, and all staff undergo regular safeguarding training. You can read our full safeguarding policy here.
