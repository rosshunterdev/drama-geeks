Terms & Conditions

Please read the following before attending Drama Geeks sessions.

Food, Drink & Allergies

  • All children must bring their own food and drink.
  • Drama Geeks is a nut-free zone.
  • No pulses, legumes or mustard products are permitted due to allergies within the group.
  • All children who require medication must have it with them at all times — this includes inhalers, epipens and antihistamines.
  • Children must bring a drink to all rehearsals. Water is best.

What to Bring

  • Scripts or handouts plus a pen or pencil at every rehearsal.
  • Dance shoes or trainers — no Crocs as they are a trip hazard.
  • Keep personal belongings to a minimum and avoid bringing toys or anything distracting.

Mobile Phones

  • Phones are permitted but must not be used during rehearsals or performances.
  • Phones may only be used during break times.
  • Misuse will result in the phone being confiscated until the end of the session.
  • Students must not record other students — this is a safeguarding risk.
  • Videos shared for rehearsal purposes must comply with our Child Protection Policy and must not be shared on social media.

Communication

  • Important messages must be texted to Charlotte in advance of rehearsals: 07792018113

Collection

  • On Saturdays, children must be collected from Dalton 4.
  • Please do not take children out of the line — they are being chaperoned from various parts of the building to ensure safe handover.

If you have any questions about these terms, please get in touch.
