What happens

What to expect

A New Musical Medley

Each week brings a brand new musical piece to learn - so every session is a fresh challenge with new songs, new energy and new excitement.

Roles From a Hat

Every role is drawn out of a hat - it's fair, fun, and means students get to explore different parts each week. No typecasting here!

Rehearse & Perfect

Students spend the session learning their parts, rehearsing together and pulling the whole piece into shape - all in one fun day.

Parents Watch the Finale

At the end of every session, parents are invited in to watch the finished performance - a proud moment every single week.

Booking

How to book

Sessions can be booked individually or as a bundle of 4 for anyone wanting to do them all. Just drop Charlotte a message to grab your slot.

Summer Saturday Workshops are held at The Westovian Theatre. Open to all - a great way to try Drama Geeks for the first time or keep creativity going over the summer.

Frequently asked questions

Can we book just one session?

Yes - sessions can be booked individually, or as a bundle of 4 if you'd like to do them all.

What should my child bring?

Comfortable clothes they can move in and a drink. All materials are provided.

Is it suitable for beginners?

Absolutely - workshops are designed to be welcoming for all experience levels, whether your child is brand new or an existing Drama Geeks student.
