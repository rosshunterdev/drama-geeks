OUR CLASSES

A class for every age.

From first-timers finding their feet to confident performers preparing for the stage — every group is taught by industry-experienced tutors.

MINI GEEKS
4–6

First Steps

Playful introduction to drama through games, storytelling and movement. Building confidence one giggle at a time.

Saturday mornings 45 minutes Cleadon Methodist
JUNIOR GEEKS
7–10

Stage & Script

Acting technique, singing and movement. Students perform in our annual showcase at The Customs House.

Saturday mornings 90 minutes The Customs House
SENIOR GEEKS
11–14

Musical Theatre

Full triple-threat training: acting, singing, dance. Students star in full-scale productions each term.

Saturday afternoons 2 hours The Westovian
ELITE GEEKS
15–18

Performance Academy

Audition-ready training for students considering drama school or industry work. LAMDA exams available.

Saturday afternoons 2.5 hours The Westovian
