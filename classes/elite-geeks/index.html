Performance academy — industry-standard training for those serious about the stage, drama school, or the profession.

What they'll learn

Performance academy training

  • 🎭

    Advanced Acting Technique

    Character development, text analysis, Stanislavski technique and contemporary approaches used in professional performance.

  • 🎵

    Advanced Vocal Training

    Extended range, belt technique, head voice and musical theatre styles — preparing for auditions and high-level performance.

  • 💃

    Dance & Physicality

    Jazz, contemporary, musical theatre and audition choreography. Physical storytelling as a professional performance tool.

  • 📋

    LAMDA Examinations

    Optional LAMDA exam preparation — nationally recognised qualifications that strengthen UCAS applications and drama school auditions.

  • 🎬

    Audition Preparation

    How to prepare, present and perform audition pieces — essential for drama school entry and professional casting situations.

What to expect

A typical Elite Geeks session

Professional Warm-up

A full physical and vocal warm-up reflecting the standard routine expected in professional rehearsal rooms.

Technique Workshop

Deep-dive into a specific area — acting method, vocal technique or physical performance — with practical exercises and individual feedback.

Production Rehearsal

Scene work, musical numbers and choreography for the term's production, run with professional rehearsal etiquette.

Audition & LAMDA Prep

One-to-one and small-group work on audition pieces and LAMDA exam texts — tailored to each student's individual goals.
