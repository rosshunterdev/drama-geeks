WHAT THEY'LL LEARN

Professional-level skills.

  • Professional audition technique
  • LAMDA examination preparation (Grades 6–8)
  • Advanced vocal coaching and performance
  • Choreography and physical theatre
  • Devising and directing fundamentals

A TYPICAL SESSION

What happens in class?

Individual Check-in
Short one-to-one with the tutor on individual development goals and LAMDA progress.
Technique Workshop
In-depth work on a specific performance skill — Stanislavski, Brecht, or physical technique.
Rehearsal or Audition Prep
Production rehearsal, monologue work, or mock audition practice depending on the term.
Reflection
Critical self-reflection, peer feedback, and tutor notes — the habits of a professional.
