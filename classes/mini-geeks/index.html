Ages 2–5

Mini Geeks

First steps in drama — where confidence, creativity and giggles all come first.

What they'll learn

Skills we build together

  • 🎭

    Dramatic Play

    Using imagination and storytelling to explore characters and scenarios in a safe, playful space.

  • 🎤

    Vocal Confidence

    Learning to use their voice, express emotions through sound and song, and begin to project with confidence.

  • 🕺

    Movement & Rhythm

    Simple movement and dance exercises that develop coordination, spatial awareness and body confidence.

  • 🤝

    Listening & Teamwork

    Group activities that encourage taking turns, active listening, and working together to create something wonderful.

  • ⭐

    Self-Confidence

    Every session celebrates effort over perfection — children leave feeling proud of what they've achieved.

What to expect

A typical Mini Geeks session

Warm-up Games

Fun drama games to get everyone moving and focused — setting a positive, energetic tone for the session.

Storytelling & Characters

Children explore a story together, taking on characters and bringing scenes to life through guided imagination.

Movement & Song

A short song or movement activity that combines rhythm, performance and a lot of energy.

Show & Share

Children share what they've created with the group — a confidence-building moment that ends every session on a high.
