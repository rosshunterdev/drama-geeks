Mini Geeks

Musical Theatre fun for little performers aged 2–5. Different themes each week with favourite Disney and Musical Theatre songs, props and dress-ups - grown-ups stay and join in!

What they'll learn

Skills we build together

  • 🎭

    Dramatic Play

    Using imagination and storytelling to explore characters and scenarios in a safe, playful space.

  • 🎤

    Vocal Confidence

    Learning to use their voice, express emotions through sound and song, and begin to project with confidence.

  • 🕺

    Movement & Rhythm

    Simple movement and dance exercises that develop coordination, spatial awareness and body confidence.

  • 🤝

    Listening & Teamwork

    Group activities that encourage taking turns, active listening, and working together to create something wonderful.

  • ⭐

    Self-Confidence

    Every session celebrates effort over perfection - children leave feeling proud of what they've achieved.

What to expect

A typical Mini Musical Theatre session

Warm-up Games

Musical Theatre warm-ups and games to get everyone moving and introduce the week's theme - energetic, fun and full of giggles.

Songs & Routines

Learning songs and simple routines from favourite Musicals and Disney films - from Let It Go to Popular, complete with props and dress-ups!

Games & Activities

Engaging activities that bring the week's Musical Theatre theme to life through play, movement and imagination.

Show & Share

Children share what they've learned with the group - a confidence-building moment that ends every session on a high.

Your tutor

Meet the teacher

Macie Sinclair

Mini Musical Theatre Tutor

Macie is a senior member of Drama Geeks and teaching assistant whose love for musical theatre started at a young age. At Drama Geeks she has recently played Elle Woods in Legally Blonde, Elsa in Frozen and Miss Honey in Matilda. Macie has received several NODA nominations and vocal championship titles, and has a wealth of knowledge and passion for performing arts that she can't wait to share with the next generation!
