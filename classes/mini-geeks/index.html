WHAT THEY'LL LEARN

Skills for life, not just the stage.

  • Listening and taking turns
  • Using imagination and creativity
  • Building confidence in front of others
  • Simple character and role-play
  • Movement, rhythm and coordination

A TYPICAL SESSION

What happens in class?

Warm-up Games
Fun, energetic games to settle nerves and get everyone focused and smiling.
Story Time
A short story or theme gives the session a thread — children bring it to life through movement and voice.
Drama Activity
Role-play, mime or simple improvisation. Structured enough for learning, free enough for fun.
Cool-down & Share
A gentle wind-down where children share what they've made. Building pride and articulacy.
