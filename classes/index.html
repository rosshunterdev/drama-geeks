Our Classes

Four age groups. One family. Find the right class for your child.

At Drama Geeks every class is built around one idea: every child deserves a place to shine. Our four age-based groups provide a clear progression from playful early-years drama all the way to audition-ready performance training — taught by industry-experienced tutors in a safe, nurturing environment.

No prior experience is needed. Every child is welcome, at any point in the year.
