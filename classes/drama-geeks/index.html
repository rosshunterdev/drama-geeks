Drama Geeks

Our flagship class - acting, singing and movement in a fun, mixed-age group that performs on real stages every term.

Drama Geeks is our heart - a mixed-age class where children aged 4 to 15 learn together, support each other, and build real performance skills in a safe, joyful environment.

No experience is needed. Children join at any point in the year and go at their own pace. Sessions run on Saturdays at The Customs House, South Shields, and Thursdays at Cleadon Methodist Church.

What they'll learn

Skills we build together

  • 🎭

    Acting Technique

    Character work, improvisation, script reading and performance - building confidence on stage through play and practice.

  • 🎤

    Singing & Vocal Work

    Group singing, breath control and vocal projection - from simple songs to full musical theatre numbers.

  • 💃

    Movement & Dance

    Choreography, physical storytelling and performance movement - skills that translate across every discipline.

  • 🤝

    Teamwork & Ensemble

    Collaboration, listening and trust - the essential skills that make great performers and great people.

  • ⭐

    Confidence & Self-Expression

    Every session celebrates effort. Children leave feeling seen, proud, and excited to come back next week.

What to expect

A typical Drama Geeks session

Warm-up

Drama games and vocal warm-ups to get the group energised, focused and ready to perform.

Skills Workshop

Focused work on acting, singing or movement - technique that feeds directly into the term's production.

Production Rehearsal

Scene work, songs and choreography for the term's show - real rehearsal process, real performance standards.

Cool-down & Share

A moment to reflect, celebrate what was made, and wind down as a group before the session ends.
