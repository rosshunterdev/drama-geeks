Ages 7–10

Junior Geeks

Stage and script training — where emerging performers find their craft and take on the stage.

What they'll learn

Skills we build together

  • 🎭

    Acting Technique

    Introduction to characterisation, script reading and performance technique in a structured, encouraging environment.

  • 🎵

    Singing

    Vocal warm-ups, group songs and solo performance to build range, confidence and stage presence.

  • 💃

    Movement & Dance

    Choreography and movement work that develops physical confidence and expression on stage.

  • 📜

    Script Work

    Learning to read, understand and interpret scripts — a foundational skill for all performance work.

  • 🌟

    Stage Confidence

    Regular performance opportunities that build the resilience and poise to shine in front of an audience.

What to expect

A typical Junior Geeks session

Vocal Warm-up

Voice exercises and breathing techniques to prepare for singing and projection work throughout the session.

Acting Workshop

Scene work and improvisation exercises building core acting skills in a fun, collaborative atmosphere.

Song & Movement

Rehearsing songs and choreography for the term's showcase, combining vocals and movement into polished numbers.

Showcase Rehearsal

As the term builds, full run-throughs of the end-of-term performance — with notes, refinements and real theatrical excitement.
