WHAT THEY'LL LEARN

More than just drama.

  • Acting technique and character work
  • Voice projection and diction
  • Singing basics and ensemble singing
  • Stage presence and performance confidence
  • Script reading and script work

A TYPICAL SESSION

What happens in class?

Warm-up
Voice and body warm-up exercises to prepare for performance work.
Skill-Building
Focused work on one technique — acting, voice, movement or singing.
Scene Work
Working in pairs or small groups on scripted or devised scenes.
Performance & Feedback
Sharing work with the group, building confidence in performance and constructive response.
