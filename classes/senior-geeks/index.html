Ages 11–14

Senior Geeks

Musical theatre — triple-threat training with full-scale productions on a real stage, every term.

What they'll learn

Triple-threat training

  • 🎭

    Acting

    Character development, scene study, emotional recall and performance technique drawn from industry-standard methods.

  • 🎵

    Singing

    Vocal technique, range development, harmony work and musical theatre repertoire — from warm-up to performance-ready.

  • 💃

    Dance

    Jazz, contemporary and musical theatre choreography — building technical skill and stage presence simultaneously.

  • 🎬

    Production Experience

    Students experience the full production process — from first read-through to opening night at The Westovian Theatre.

What to expect

A typical Senior Geeks session

Physical & Vocal Warm-up

A structured warm-up covering both body and voice, preparing students for the physical demands of a full rehearsal session.

Acting & Scene Work

Technique workshops, improvisation and scripted scene work focused on the current term's production.

Song & Dance Rehearsal

Musical numbers rehearsed section by section — vocals, choreography and then the full number combined.

Full Run-throughs

As the term progresses, full run-throughs build stamina and performance confidence ahead of the real show.
