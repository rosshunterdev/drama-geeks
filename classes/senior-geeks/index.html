WHAT THEY'LL LEARN

Triple-threat training.

  • Advanced acting and character development
  • Vocal technique and solo singing
  • Choreography and stage movement
  • Production skills — blocking, cues, ensemble
  • LAMDA exam preparation (optional)

A TYPICAL SESSION

What happens in class?

Physical Warm-up
Full body and voice preparation — essential before intensive rehearsal.
Technique Focus
Rotating between acting, vocals and movement across the week.
Production Rehearsal
When a show is in progress, the majority of session time is rehearsal.
Notes & Review
Constructive notes from the tutor. Learning to receive feedback gracefully is a skill in itself.
