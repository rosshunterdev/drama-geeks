Discounts

We offer two types of discount - one for children doing more than one class, and one for families with more than one child enrolled.

Multi-class discount

When a child attends more than one class, their most expensive class is charged at full price and each additional class gets a bigger discount. Discounts are applied in order of class price - highest price first.

Class (by price, highest first) Discount
1st class Full price
2nd class 20% off
3rd class 30% off
4th class 40% off

Sibling discount

When you have more than one child enrolled, younger siblings receive a discount across all of their classes. The discount applies to the child's total fee.

Child Discount
1st child Full price
2nd child 20% off
3rd child and beyond 30% off

Not sure how a discount applies in your situation? Get in touch and we'll work it out for you.

How to pay

Payments

Fees are collected automatically each month through our ClassManager portal - there's nothing to remember or do manually.

Payments are taken on the 1st of each month. Rather than charging by term, your annual fee is spread evenly across the whole year, with planned breaks already factored in. That means the amount stays consistent every month - there's no extra charge for busy terms and no credit for holidays. What you see is what you pay, month in, month out.

When you enrol, we'll send you a link to set up your payments through ClassManager. If you have any questions about billing, just get in touch.
