What happens

What to expect

Games & Warm-up

We kick off every workshop day with high-energy games and warm-ups to get everyone relaxed, laughing and ready to go.

Skills Sessions

Workshops covering acting, singing, movement and improvisation — with the content tailored to the holiday theme.

Devising & Rehearsing

Students create and rehearse a short piece together across the workshop days, developing teamwork and creative skills.

Showcase Performance

On the final day, students perform what they've created for parents — a proud moment that caps off every workshop week.

Booking

How to book

Workshops can be booked by contacting us directly. Places are limited and fill quickly — we recommend booking early to avoid disappointment.

Holiday workshops are open to both existing Drama Geeks students and new joiners — they're a brilliant, low-commitment way to try out Drama Geeks for the first time.

Frequently asked questions

What should my child bring? Comfortable clothes they can move in, a packed lunch and a drink. All materials are provided.

What age are workshops for? Most workshops run for ages 4–18, though specific workshops may have narrower age ranges — check individual listings.

Is it suitable for beginners? Absolutely — holiday workshops are designed to be welcoming for all experience levels.
