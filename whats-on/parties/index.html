The experience

What happens at a Drama Geeks party

Arrival & Welcome

Our host greets the children with a fun warm-up game as they arrive — getting everyone in the mood from the very first minute.

Drama Workshop

Games, improvisation and acting exercises designed to be inclusive and hilarious — every child gets involved regardless of experience.

Performance Showcase

Children rehearse and perform a short piece together — parents and family are invited in to watch at the end.

Birthday Finale

The birthday child gets their moment in the spotlight — a special farewell that makes them feel like a true star.
